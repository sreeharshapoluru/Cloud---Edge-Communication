Device Control

Click on the get temperature to get the current temperature from the temperature sensor.

The buzzer can be switched ON and OFF by clicking on the corresponding buttons under the BUZZER section.

In the RGB section click on the specific colored button to switch on the corresponding LED.

In the LED section click on the ON or OFF button near the LED of your choice to switch ON or OFF the corresponding LED.
